Compressing Videos

First, install HandBrake a free and open source program here: https://handbrake.fr

During the installation wizard UNTICK all of the boxes, like the ones saying to disable windows snipping tool, and startup with windows etc

Now, drag your video or clip into the box.

If you click on "Preset" you will find multiple presets you can use. In this case im creating my own.

Under "Video" here are my settings. The lower the CQ number the higher the quality but, higher the file size. For video encoder choose the same H.264 or better (AMF FOR AMD). If you see neither use x264 (cpu)

Now, save your preset by clicking "Save New Preset". Finally make sure your path where it is to be saved is correct, and click on "Start Encode" and you are done.

Your compressed video or clip is saved in your selected location. Click "Open Source" to compress more
